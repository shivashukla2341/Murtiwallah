We deliver to every PIN code in India via our logistics partners. Standard shipping is free on retail orders; express delivery is available at checkout for an additional fee.

2. Export Shipping

We export to 42 countries via air and sea freight, with full customs documentation, packing lists and certificates of origin prepared by our export desk. Duties and import taxes in the destination country are the buyer's responsibility.

3. Packaging

Every idol ships in a custom foam-fitted crate or box rated for its material and weight, with fragile-handling markings. Bulk pallets are shrink-wrapped and corner-protected for freight transit.

4. Delays

Made-to-order and large temple-installation pieces may extend delivery timelines — the estimated ship date is always confirmed at order time.

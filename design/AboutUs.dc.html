हमारी कहानी — Our Story

Four Generations at the Same Workbenches

Murtiwallah began in 1968 as a single Aligarh brass workshop supplying local temples. Today the same karigar families — now grandchildren of the founders — cast, carve and finish every piece we sell, wholesaled to over 10,000 dealers and exported to 42 countries.

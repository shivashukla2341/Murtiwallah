Refund & Cancellation Policy

Last updated: 1 July 2026

1. Retail Orders

Retail (non-custom, in-stock) orders may be returned within 7 days of delivery if unused and in original packaging. Refunds are issued to the original payment method within 5–7 business days of receiving the returned item.

2. Custom & Made-to-Order Pieces

Murtis carved or cast to a custom size, design or finish are final sale once production has begun. The advance paid at order confirmation is non-refundable.

3. Wholesale & Bulk Orders

  • Cancellations are accepted only before production begins.
  • Approved samples are binding — bulk production matching the sample is not refundable for aesthetic reasons.
  • Genuine transit damage is covered — report with photos within 48 hours of delivery.

4. Damaged or Incorrect Items

If your order arrives damaged or incorrect, contact support@murtiwallah.in within 48 hours with photos. We will arrange a replacement or full refund at no cost to you.

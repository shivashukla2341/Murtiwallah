Frequently Asked Questions

Retail and wholesale questions, answered by our support desk.

Ordering & Products

Are the idols really hand-carved, or machine-made?

Every piece is hand-finished by our karigar families — casting or rough-carving may use moulds or basic tools, but every face, ornament and fold is finished entirely by hand.

Can I order a custom size or design?

Yes — use the Custom Temple Orders form on our Wholesale page with your reference photo or sketch, deity, size and material, and our design desk will respond within 2 business days.

Do you offer a warranty on metal idols?

All brass, bronze and panchdhatu pieces carry a 2-year warranty against casting defects. Marble and stone pieces are covered for structural cracks from manufacturing.

Wholesale & Bulk

What is the minimum order quantity (MOQ)?

MOQ varies by product, typically 5–50 pieces per SKU. Each product page lists its specific MOQ and tiered wholesale pricing.

Do you provide GST invoices for bulk orders?

Yes, every wholesale and export order is billed with a proper GST invoice, and we support GST number capture at checkout for business buyers.

Can I get a physical sample before a bulk order?

Yes — our dealer desk can dispatch a paid sample (adjusted against your final order) within 3–5 business days.

Shipping & Returns

Do you deliver Pan-India and export worldwide?

Yes — we ship to every Indian PIN code and export to 42 countries with full customs documentation.

What is your return policy on idols?

Retail orders can be returned within 7 days if unused and in original packaging. Custom, made-to-order and bulk wholesale orders are final sale — see our Refund Policy for details.

Privacy Policy

Last updated: 1 July 2026

1. Information We Collect

We collect the information you give us directly — name, email, phone, shipping and billing address, and business details (GST number, company name) for wholesale accounts — along with order history and, with consent, browsing behaviour to improve product recommendations.

2. How We Use Your Information

Your information is used to process orders, issue GST invoices, coordinate shipping and export documentation, respond to support requests, and — only with your opt-in — send newsletter and festival-collection updates.

3. Sharing With Third Parties

We share order data with logistics partners, payment processors and, for export orders, customs agents — only to the extent needed to fulfil your order. We do not sell personal data to third parties.

4. Data Security

Payment details are processed by PCI-DSS compliant gateways and are never stored on our servers. Account data is encrypted in transit and at rest.

5. Your Rights

You may request access to, correction of, or deletion of your personal data at any time by writing to privacy@murtiwallah.in.
